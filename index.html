Musicians & Singers

Career Overview

What Do They Do?

  • Playing musical instruments such as guitar, piano, drums, violin, etc.
  • Entertaining audiences at concerts, festivals, clubs, and other venues.
  • Collaborating with other musicians, songwriters, producers, and industry professionals.
  • Building their brand, promoting their music through social media, concerts, and other channels.

Sharing their skills and knowledge by offering music lessons or workshops.
